## 重点词汇表

| 短语 | 英文释义 | 中文释义 | 例句 |
|------|----------|----------|------|
| **float an idea** | gently suggest an idea | 委婉提出想法；试探性建议；抛出想法 | There was a spare bedroom on our family holiday, so I **floated the idea** of asking my best friend too. |
| **bat an idea around** | say something just to see what others think of the idea | 抛砖引玉；随口说说；讨论想法 | I'm not sure about changing career, I'm just **batting the idea around** to see what my friends think. |
| **put ideas into (someone's) head** | make someone think of doing something that they shouldn't really do | 给某人灌输想法；唆使；让某人动了不该有的念头 | She's got to stay in and do her homework. Don't go **putting ideas into her head** about going to the football match! |
| **that's (someone's) idea of (something)** | expression used to show someone thinks differently to you | 某人眼中的……；某人所谓的……(暗示说话者不同意) | Walking up a mountain in the rain? I'm sorry, but **that's** not **my idea of** a good time. |

## 详细解析

### 1. Float an idea
- **核心含义**：**委婉地、试探性地**提出想法，不强求采纳
- **意象**：float (漂浮) → 像气球/羽毛一样轻轻抛出去，看风向
- **语体**：半正式，职场会议、家庭讨论、头脑风暴常用
- **语法**：float an idea / float the idea of doing sth
- **同义**：put forward an idea, suggest tentatively, test the waters, air a suggestion
- **反义**：impose an idea, push through a proposal, mandate

### 2. Bat an idea around
- **核心含义**：**非正式讨论**，随口说说，不承诺、不深究，看大家反应
- **意象**：bat (击球) → 像打棒球/网球来回击球，想法在人群中来回"拍打"
- **语体**：非正式，口语化，团队氛围轻松时用
- **语法**：bat an idea around / bat around an idea
- **同义**：bounce ideas around, kick ideas around, toss ideas around, spitball (美语俚语)
- **场景**：早期创意阶段、午餐闲聊、非正式会议

### 3. Put ideas into someone's head
- **核心含义**：**负面**——让某人产生原本没有、或不该有的念头/冲动
- **语用色彩**：责备、警告、保护性语境 (父母对孩子、上级对下级、朋友间劝阻)
- **结构**：put ideas into [人]('s) head
- **常见否定劝诫**：Don't put ideas into his head!
- **同义**：plant ideas in someone's mind, give someone notions, lead someone astray
- **文化注**：英语将"想法"视为可被"植入"的实体，类似 inception (植入概念)

### 4. That's (someone's) idea of (something)
- **核心含义**：**反讽/吐槽**——某人对某事的定义/标准与常人/说话者截然不同
- **结构**：that's + [人] + 's idea of + [名词/动名词]
- **语气**：轻微嘲讽、无奈、幽默抱怨
- **例句变奏**：
  - Sleeping till noon? **That's his idea of** a productive morning.
  - Eating instant noodles three days straight? **That's her idea of** cooking.
- **同义**：what sb calls..., sb's version of..., sb's definition of...

## 完整逐字稿

**Phil**
I've just had an idea! Why don't we learn four phrases with 'idea'?

We can use **float an idea** to mean suggest an idea – gently suggest.

No one knew what to do, so I **floated the idea** of stopping and trying again tomorrow.

**Bat an idea around** – that's when you say something just to see what people think of your idea.

I'm just **batting around an idea**, but why don't we try using AI?

When you **put ideas into someone's head**, you make them think of doing something, and often that's something they shouldn't really do.

Don't go **putting ideas into my head**. I'm not spending all my money on that!

If you say **that's his idea of** hard work, or anything else, he thinks it's hard work – you probably don't. We use it when we think differently about something.

Sorry, **that's** just **his idea of** a joke, but it's not funny.

Let me **float an idea**. I think you should try learning these phrases.

## 批判性思考题

1. **文化隐喻**：为什么英语用 "party" 衍生出这么多社交隐喻？中文里有哪些用 "局"、"场"、"饭" 衍生的类似表达？
2. **褒贬辨析**："Party animal" 总是褒义吗？什么语境下可能带负面评价（如：不务正业、酗酒）？
3. **性别视角**："Life and soul of the party" 形容女性时是否存在刻板印象？如何中性表达"社交达人"？
4. **技术采用曲线**："Late to the party" 对应技术采用生命周期的哪个阶段？早期采用者 vs 滞后者的优劣势？
5. **个人盘点**：你身边谁是 party animal / life and soul of the party？你的 party trick 是什么？最近一次 late to the party 是什么时候？
