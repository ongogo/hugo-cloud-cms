## 详细解析

### 1. Party animal
- **含义**：指非常喜欢参加聚会、经常去聚会且玩得很开心的人。
- **语气**：非正式、略带赞赏或调侃。
- **同义**：social butterfly, party-goer, clubber
- **反义**：wallflower (墙花，指社交场合不合群者), homebody (宅家的人)

### 2. The life and soul of the party
- **含义**：让聚会变得更有趣的人；气氛组核心。
- **变体**：the life of the party (美式更常用)
- **文化点**：英式英语常说 "life and soul"，美式常省略 "and soul"。
- **同义**：the center of attention, the entertainer

### 3. Party trick
- **含义**：你会做而别人不会的事，可在聚会上表演给人看的绝活。
- **引申**：不一定非得在派对上；任何"看家本领"均可称 party trick。
- **例子**：倒立、变魔术、模仿口音、速解魔方、背圆周率等。

### 4. Late to the party
- **含义**：比喻对某事物（剧集、潮流、技术、梗）了解得比别人晚。
- **字面 vs 比喻**：字面指迟到派对；实际常指"晚入坑"、"补课"。
- **自嘲常用语**："I know I'm late to the party, but..."
- **同义**：behind the curve, out of the loop, playing catch-up

## 完整逐字稿

**Phil**
It's time to party! By party, I mean learn four phrases with 'party'.

Someone who loves parties, goes to lots of them and really enjoys themselves is a **party animal**.

Of course she'll come – she's a real **party animal**.

Some people really make a party more fun. And if you're that person, you might be **the life and soul of the party**.

He's great to be around. He makes everyone laugh. He's **the life and soul of the party**.

A **party trick** is something you can do and not many other people can. And you can use it to impress people at a party.

Doing perfect handstands was her **party trick**.

Someone who says they're **late to the party** might not actually be talking about a party. We use it to say we found out about, or got interested in, something long after everyone else.

I know I'm **late to the party**, but I loved that series from last year.

Don't be **late to the party** – learn these phrases today!

## 批判性思考题

1. **文化隐喻**：为什么英语用 "party" 衍生出这么多社交隐喻？中文里有哪些用 "局"、"场"、"饭" 衍生的类似表达？
2. **褒贬辨析**："Party animal" 总是褒义吗？什么语境下可能带负面评价（如：不务正业、酗酒）？
3. **性别视角**："Life and soul of the party" 形容女性时是否存在刻板印象？如何中性表达"社交达人"？
4. **技术采用曲线**："Late to the party" 对应技术采用生命周期的哪个阶段？早期采用者 vs 滞后者的优劣势？
5. **个人盘点**：你身边谁是 party animal / life and soul of the party？你的 party trick 是什么？最近一次 late to the party 是什么时候？
