## 重点词汇表

| 短语 | 英文释义 | 中文释义 | 例句 |
|------|----------|----------|------|
| **no big deal** | not important | 没什么大不了的；无所谓；小事一桩 | She forgot to buy balloons for the party but it's **no big deal**. The venue already looks great. |
| **a done deal** | a finalised plan or agreement that won't change | 定局；板上钉钉的事；已敲定的协议 | He's thinking about investing in the company, but it's far from being **a done deal**. |
| **a deal breaker** | something that makes you not continue with an agreement or relationship | 决定性因素；障碍；让人退出的原因 | I'm looking for a flatmate, but if they're messy, then that's **a deal breaker** for me. |
| **a great deal** | a lot | 很多；大量 | I have **a great deal** of love and respect for my parents. |

## 详细解析

### 1. No big deal
- **核心含义**：表示某事**不重要、不严重、不必在意**
- **语体**：非正式，口语极常用
- **简略形式**：**No biggie** (更口语化)
- **常用场景**：安慰道歉者、淡化小错误、表达宽容
- **同义**：not a problem, nothing to worry about, it's fine, never mind
- **反义**：a big deal (很重要的事), a major issue

### 2. A done deal
- **核心含义**：**已最终确定、不可更改**的计划或协议
- **意象**：deal (交易) + done (完成) → 尘埃落定
- **常用否定**：**far from a done deal** (远未定局、还有变数)
- **同义**：a sure thing, settled, finalized, in the bag, a foregone conclusion
- **反义**：up in the air, tentative, pending, undecided

### 3. A deal breaker
- **核心含义**：**让你决定不继续**协议、关系、谈判的关键因素
- **词源**：break + deal → 打破交易的东西
- **应用场景**：
  - 租房/合租：messy, noisy, pets, smoking
  - 恋爱/婚姻：dishonesty, different values, kids/no kids
  - 求职：salary, location, work-life balance, culture
  - 商务谈判：IP ownership, liability, exclusivity
- **同义**：deal-breaker (连字符), showstopper, non-starter, red line
- **反义**：deal maker, selling point, non-issue

### 4. A great deal
- **核心含义**：**大量、很多** (= a lot, lots of, a large amount of)
- **语法重点**：
  - 修饰**不可数名词**：a great deal of time / money / effort / love / respect
  - **不可**修饰可数名词复数 (✗ a great deal of books → ✓ a great many books / a large number of books)
- **语体**：稍正式、书面、文学色彩
- **同义**：a large amount of, a lot of, lots of, plenty of, a wealth of
- **常见搭配**：a great deal of time/effort/money/attention/interest/difficulty/trouble

## 完整逐字稿

**Georgie**
Let's learn four phrases with 'deal'.

If something is **no big deal**, it's not important. You can also say 'no biggie' for short.

Don't worry about being late. It's **no big deal** – no biggie.

If something is **a done deal**, it means a plan or agreement is finalised and won't change.

After the final interview went well, she knew the job was **a done deal**.

**A deal breaker** is something that makes you not continue with an agreement or relationship.

A lack of honesty and open communication is **a deal breaker** for him. He won't be friends with someone who lies.

And lastly, **a great deal** of something is another way of saying a lot of something. It's typically used with uncountable nouns.

He spends **a great deal** of time lying on the sofa watching TV.

It takes **a great deal** of effort to learn English, so don't just watch this video – try writing your own example sentences using these phrases.

## 批判性思考题

1. **语域标量**：no big deal (极非正式) < a done deal (半正式) < a deal breaker (半正式) < a great deal (正式/书面)。同一词根 deal 衍生出不同语域表达，反映英语词汇的"语域分层"特征。
2. **隐喻一致性**：deal 本义"交易、协议"，四短语均保留交易框架：no big deal (小交易不算数)、done deal (交易完成)、deal breaker (破坏交易)、great deal (大量交易/份量)。
3. **否定极性**：no big deal / far from a done deal / a deal breaker —— 三个表达中两个含否定，一个含"破坏"义。英语倾向用否定式表达边界条件。
4. **可数性陷阱**：a great deal of + 不可数名词。学习者易误用于可数名词。设计练习：改错句 "a great deal of people / problems / ideas"。
5. **跨文化谈判**：中式谈判常说"底线"、"红线"对应 deal breaker。但 deal breaker 更具体、可枚举、可协商。如何用英语表达"这是我的底线" vs "这只是个顾虑"？
