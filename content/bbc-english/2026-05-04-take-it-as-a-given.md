本集节目讲解英语短语 **"take it as a given"** 的用法。这个表达用于描述那些我们可以默认认为理所当然的事情，即使没有完全确认。Feifei 和 Phil 通过对话和例子，展示了如何在日常生活中使用这个地道短语。

---

## Vocabulary

| 单词/短语 | 英文解释 | 中文解释 |
|--------------------------|-----------------------------------------------|------------------------------|
| **take it as a given**   | assume something is true without full certainty | 默认，理所当然              |
| **take it as read**      | accept something as true without verification  | 无需验证即接受              |
| **assume**               | accept something as true without proof         | 假设，设想                  |
| **forecast**             | prediction of future weather                  | 天气预报                    |

---

## Examples

1. This work is for our most important client, so I'll **take it as a given** that you've checked everything really carefully.
2. If you're planning to drive somewhere nice on a sunny weekend, you should **take it as a given** that there'll be traffic jams.
3. If you change your computer password at work, you can **take it as a given** that it will take you two weeks to memorize it.

---

## Common Mistakes

❌ *I take it as given the project will be delayed.*
✅ **Correct**: *I take it as **a** given that the project will be delayed.* (the "a" cannot be dropped)

❌ *You can take for granted that she'll be late.*
✅ **Better**: *You can take it as a given that she'll be late.* (take for granted stresses "not appreciating", while take it as a given stresses "assuming")
