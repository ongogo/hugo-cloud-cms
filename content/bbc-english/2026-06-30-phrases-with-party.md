## Key Vocabulary

| 短语 | 英文释义 | 中文释义 | 例句 |
|------|----------|----------|------|
| **party animal** | someone who loves parties | 爱参加聚会的人 | Of course she'll come – she's a real **party animal**. |
| **the life and soul of the party** | someone who makes a party more fun | 聚会的开心果 | He makes everyone laugh. He's **the life and soul of the party**. |
| **party trick** | something you can do that other people can't | 绝活；拿手好戏 | Doing perfect handstands was her **party trick**. |
| **late to the party** | find out about something long after everyone else | 慢半拍；晚入坑 | I know I'm **late to the party**, but I loved that series from last year. |

## Transcript

**Phil**: It's time to party! By party, I mean learn four phrases with 'party'.

Someone who loves parties, goes to lots of them and really enjoys themselves is a **party animal**.

Some people really make a party more fun. If you're that person, you might be **the life and soul of the party**.

A **party trick** is something you can do that not many other people can — you can use it to impress people at a party.

Someone who says they're **late to the party** might not actually be talking about a party. We use it to say we found out about, or got interested in, something long after everyone else.

Don't be **late to the party** — learn these phrases today!
