## 核心词汇 Vocabulary

| Word | English Definition | Chinese Definition | Source |

## 对话要点 Transcript Highlights

### 1. 气候变化 Climate Change
> **Neil**: *"Climate change is the change in weather over a long period of time. And when we talk about climate change now, we're talking mainly about the world getting hotter."*
>
> **Becca**: *"Experts say that this could be because of **greenhouse gases**."*

**解析**：
- **climate change** = 长期天气变化，现多指全球变暖
- **over a long period of time** = 在很长一段时间内（固定搭配）

### 2. 温室气体 Greenhouse Gases
> **Becca**: *"**Greenhouse gases**, like carbon dioxide for example, they stay in the Earth's atmosphere and they trap the sun's heat in the atmosphere, meaning that the Earth gets hotter and hotter."*
>
> **Neil**: *"And so, one thing that scientists say is that we should burn fewer **fossil fuels**."*

**解析**：
- **trap** = 困住、锁住（温室效应核心动词）
- **stay in the atmosphere** = 停留在大气层中

### 3. 化石燃料 Fossil Fuels
> **Neil**: *"**Fossil fuels** are things like oil and gas that come from the Earth. And when you burn them, they create carbon dioxide – CO2."*

**解析**：
- **come from the Earth** = 来自地球（地下开采）
- **burn** = 燃烧（释放 CO2）

### 4. 可再生能源 Renewable Energy
> **Becca**: *"A lot of people are using **renewable energy**. So, **renewable energy** is energy that comes from things like the wind or from the sun that aren't likely to run out."*

**解析**：
- **run out** = 用完、耗尽（ phrasal verb 重点）
- **aren't likely to** = 不大可能（表概率）

### 5. 节约能源与水 Energy & Water Conservation
> **Neil**: *"**Energy conservation** is also really important... that means not using too much energy, especially if you don't really need to... And we can also talk about **water conservation** and that means not using too much water."*
>
> **Becca**: *"OK. So, like when I turn the tap off when I brush my teeth."*

**解析**：
- **conservation** = 保护、节约（名词）
- **conserve** = 节约、保护（动词）
- **turn the tap off** = 关水龙头（实用口语）

### 6. 词汇回顾 Recap
> **Neil**: *"**Fossil fuels** are things like oil, gas and coal, which we burn to get energy. And when we burn them, they release carbon into the atmosphere."*
>
> **Becca**: *"Carbon dioxide is one of the gases that we call **greenhouse gases**. Now, **greenhouse gases** are those gases that keep the heat inside the Earth's atmosphere."*
>
> **Neil**: *"We also heard **energy conservation**, and that means using less energy. **Conservation** means protecting something, so you can also **conserve water** and that means not using too much water if you don't have to."*
>
> **Becca**: *"And lastly, **renewable energy**, and this is the energy that we can make from things like the wind, the sun or the movement of water."*

## 语言特点 Language Features

### 一般现在时 Present Simple (事实/习惯)
- *"Climate change **is** the change in weather..."*
- *"Greenhouse gases **stay** in the Earth's atmosphere..."*
- *"They **trap** the sun's heat..."*
- *"Fossil fuels **come** from the Earth."*

### 情态动词 Modal Verbs (建议/可能性)
- *"We **should** burn fewer fossil fuels."*
- *"That **means** not using too much energy."*
- *"Things that **aren't likely to** run out."*

### 被动语态 Passive Voice (客观陈述)
- *"They **are used** to get energy."*
- *"Carbon dioxide **is produced** by burning fossil fuels."*

### 实用口语表达 Useful Spoken Phrases
- *"It's an interesting topic, isn't it?"* — 寻求共鸣/开启话题
- *"I remember a few years ago..."* — 讲述个人经历
- *"That is something I have never experienced in my life."* — 强调前所未有
- *"Yeah, it was."* — 简短回应表示同意
- *"Exactly."* — 精确确认
- *"Let's recap..."* — 总结重点
- *"That's it for this episode..."* — 结束语

## 批判性思考 Critical Thinking Questions

1. **自我反思**: Have you noticed climate change where you live? What extreme weather have you experienced?
2. **习惯分析**: What do you do to **conserve energy** or **conserve water** at home? (turn off taps, use public transport, lower heating...)
3. **能源转型**: **Renewable energy** (wind, solar) vs **fossil fuels** (coal, oil). What are the pros and cons of each? Can your country run on 100% renewables?
4. **个人责任 vs 系统改变**: Neil says "not having your heating turned up too high." Becca says "turn the tap off when I brush my teeth." Are individual actions enough? What systemic changes are needed?
5. **词汇应用**: Try explaining "greenhouse gases" 和 "fossil fuels" 的关系。用英语向朋友解释为什么燃烧化石燃料导致全球变暖。
6. **未来展望**: What do you think the climate will be like in 20 years? Will **energy conservation** become more important?

## 学习建议 Study Tips

- **跟读练习**: 选择一段对话（如 Neil 解释 fossil fuels），模仿语音语调反复跟读 3-5 遍
- **词汇卡片**: 为 6 个核心词汇制作卡片，正面写英语，背面写中文定义 + 例句
- **口语输出**: 尝试用英语回答：*"What is climate change?"* *"What are fossil fuels?"* *"How can we conserve energy?"*
- **写作练习**: 写一段 80-100 词的短文，描述你所在地区的气候变化现象和你采取的环保行动
- **真实应用**: 打开手机天气/新闻 App，找一篇关于气候变化的英文新闻，标记出本课学到的词汇
- **扩展学习**: 搜索 *"carbon footprint"* *"net zero"* *"climate action"* 相关英语文章/视频
