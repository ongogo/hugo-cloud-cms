## 核心词汇 Vocabulary

| Word | English Definition | Chinese Definition | Source |
|------|-------------------|-------------------|--------|
| **screen time** | time spent looking at an electronic screen, often your phone screen | 屏幕使用时间，看屏幕的时间 | Neil & Becca |
| **doomscrolling** | using your phone to look at one negative story after another for a long time, making yourself feel unhappy | 厄运滚动，刷坏消息（指连续浏览负面新闻导致情绪低落） | Neil & Becca |
| **notifications** | messages that apps use to encourage you to check your phone | 通知，推送消息 | Neil & Becca |
| **addiction** | habit or activity that you cannot easily stop doing | 成瘾，上瘾 | Neil & Becca |
| **handy** | useful, convenient | 方便的，顺手的 | Neil |
| **waste of time** | something that uses time but gives no benefit | 浪费时间 | Neil |
| **scroll** | move text or images on a screen up or down | 滚动（屏幕） | Neil & Becca |
| **setting** | an option you can change in an app or device | 设置 | Neil & Becca |
| **limit** | a maximum amount allowed | 限制，限额 | Neil & Becca |
| **distraction** | something that takes your attention away from what you're doing | 分心的事物，干扰 | Neil & Becca |

## 对话要点 Transcript Highlights

### 1. 屏幕时间 Screen Time
> **Neil**: "Yeah, I spend probably a lot of time on my phone every day, and I'm not really happy about that because I think it's a waste of time."
>
> **Becca**: "So, your **screen time** is the time in which you spend looking on your phone screen. I probably would guess that I spend quite a few hours looking at my phone."

### 2. 厄运滚动 Doomscrolling
> **Neil**: "Do you ever do **doomscrolling**?"
>
> **Becca**: "**Doomscrolling** is when you look at one bad news story after another... you just keep scrolling and scrolling, and you feel worse and worse."

### 3. 通知成瘾 Notifications & Addiction
> **Neil**: "I get so many **notifications**... it's like an **addiction**."
>
> **Becca**: "Apps send **notifications** to make you check your phone. It's designed to be addictive."

### 4. 设置限制 Setting Limits
> **Neil**: "I've set a **limit** on my social media apps... one hour a day."
>
> **Becca**: "That's a good idea. I should check my **settings** and set some **limits** too."

### 5. 专注模式 Focus Modes
> **Neil**: "I use **focus mode** when I'm working... it stops **distractions**."
>
> **Becca**: "Maybe I need to try that. My phone is such a **distraction** when I'm trying to study."

## 语言特点 Language Features

### 现在完成时 Present Perfect
- "I **haven't checked** my screen time in a while"
- "I **haven't set** any limits yet"

### 情态动词 Modal Verbs for Advice
- "You **should** check your settings"
- "I **could** try focus mode"
- "It **might** help"

### 动名词作主语 Gerunds as Subjects
- "**Scrolling** makes me feel tired"
- "**Checking** notifications is a habit"

### 实用口语表达 Useful Spoken Phrases
- "Ooh, I don't know the number" — 含糊回答
- "Quite a few hours" — 模糊数量
- "I'm not really happy about that" — 委婉表达不满
- "Like millions of other people" — 寻求共鸣
- "Mmm, yeah" — 表示赞同/思考
- "That's a good idea" — 肯定建议

## 批判性思考 Critical Thinking Questions

1. **自我反思**: How many hours a day do you spend on your phone? Have you ever checked your "screen time" settings?
2. **习惯分析**: Do you ever do "doomscrolling"? What triggers it for you — bad news, social media, or something else?
3. **设计伦理**: Apps are deliberately designed to be addictive (notifications, infinite scroll, streaks). Is this ethical? Should there be regulation?
4. **解决方案**: Neil uses "focus mode" and app limits. What strategies have you tried to reduce phone usage? What worked / didn't work?
5. **代际差异**: Do you think younger generations have a different relationship with phones than older generations? How?
6. **平衡**: Phones are also "handy" for information, maps, payments, communication. How do you balance utility vs. distraction?

## 学习建议 Study Tips

- **跟读练习**: 选择一段对话（如Neil问Becca屏幕时间的部分），模仿语音语调反复跟读 3-5 遍
- **词汇卡片**: 为 4 个核心词汇制作卡片，正面写英语，背面写中文定义 + 例句
- **口语输出**: 尝试用英语回答："How much screen time do you have?" "Do you doomscroll?" "What notifications do you get?"
- **写作练习**: 写一段 80-100 词的短文，描述你的手机使用习惯和你想做的改变
- **真实应用**: 打开手机的"屏幕使用时间"统计，用英语记录一周的数据变化
- **扩展学习**: 搜索 "digital wellbeing" "phone addiction" "dopamine detox" 相关英语文章/视频
