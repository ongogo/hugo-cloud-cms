## Content Summary

The United States has moved to implement a blockade of Iranian ports in the Strait of Hormuz after US-Iran peace talks collapsed over the weekend. Trump warned that any ships approaching the blockade would be "immediately ELIMINATED" using military force. US Central Command issued a formal notice to seafarers outlining enforcement measures, though questions remain about how fully the blockade can be enforced. Despite the tension, Pakistan continues diplomatic efforts as the two-week ceasefire remains fragile.
