## 📰 文章摘要 (Article Summary)
近期热映的纪录片《丝路》聚焦一幅宋代古地图——它描绘的疆域与航线远超西方记载，引发学界与网络热烈讨论。支持者认为该地图证明宋代中国已掌握全球地理知识，动摇“大航海时代欧洲中心论”；批评者则指其存在夸大成分，斥为民族主义宣传工具。无论立场如何，这部纪录片已成功将“历史话语权之争”这一议题推向公共视野。

## 🔗 短语动词 & 高分连接器

### 🟢 Phrasal Verbs
1. **date back (to)** → 追溯至
   - *This map **dates back** to the Song dynasty.*
2. **pit ... against ...** → 使竞争；使对立
   - *The documentary **pits** Chinese cartography **against** European records.*
3. **tap into** → 引发；触动（情绪/热点）
   - *The film **taps into** national pride and academic debate alike.*
4. **spark ... debate** → 引发争论
   - *It **sparked** heated **debate** on social media.*

### 🔵 High-end Connectors
1. **„Regardless of..., ...“** → 无论…都…
   - *Regardless of the historical accuracy, the film has reshaped public discourse.*
2. **„In an attempt to..., ...“** → 为了…（表目的）
   - *In an attempt to reclaim historical narratives, Beijing funds more archaeological research.*
3. **„The more ..., the more ...“** → 越…越…（经典高级句式）
   - *The more the map is studied, the more questions arise.*

### ✏️ Sentence Building
1. 这幅地图 ________（date back）到明代初年，描绘的航线范围令人震惊。
2. 该纪录片 ________（spark）社交媒体上关于历史话语权的激烈 ________（debate）。
3. ________（Regardless of）学术争议，影片已成功将传统文化议题推向公共视野。

## 🧠 Teacher's Tip for Yoyo
写作文讨论“历史争议”类话题，可用：
> *Regardless of whether the map is 100% accurate, it has reignited a global conversation about who writes history — and why that question matters today.*
开篇先 **"Regardless of..."** 或 **"In an attempt to..."**，瞬间提升 topic-sentence 的学术感！
另外注意 **subsequent / narrative / territorial claim** 这三个词——都是雅思/高考写作描述外交、政策争议的常用词，务必熟记！✍️
