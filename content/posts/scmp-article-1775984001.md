## 中文摘要 (Chinese Summary)
日本最近进行了军事演习，显示出其对中国的威慑焦点正在增加。演习名为Salaknib，将持续至4月17日，随后将进行更大规模的Balikatan美菲联合演习。这是自二战以来日本军队首次在菲律宾部署。这些演习反映了日本在印太地区防御策略的重大转变，旨在应对中国日益增长的军事存在和影响力。

## Content Summary
Japan has deployed troops to the Philippines for military drills, marking the first such deployment since World War II. The Salaknib exercise runs until April 17, followed by the larger Balakatan US-Philippines exercises involving all military branches. These drills demonstrate Tokyo's growing focus on deterring China through enhanced military cooperation in the Indo-Pacific region. The exercises represent a significant shift in Japan's defense posture, moving beyond its post-war pacifist constraints to actively counter China's military expansion.
