## English Summary
Feng shui master Andrew Kwan explains that the cosmic energy for the third lunar month of the Year of the Horse shifts to a water pillar, which clashes with the year's fire sign. This transition brings a period of change and purification, where stability and a methodical approach are essential for breaking through predicaments and avoiding financial fluctuations.

## 中文总结
风水师 Andrew Kwan 指出，马年第三月的宇宙能量转向“水柱”，这与年度的“火”象产生冲突。整体而言，本月将是一个充满变化与净化的时期。对于成熟且谨慎的人来说，有望获得财富和权力，但所有人需警惕财务波动以及合同文件中的重大错误。保持现状、稳扎稳打将是突破困境的关键。

## Article

Feng shui master Andrew Kwan says that cosmic energy shifts to a water pillar this month, which clashes with the fire sign of the year. In broader terms, this will be a month of change and purification.

Those who are mature and prudent will gain wealth and authority, but all must be wary of financial fluctuations and major errors in documents and contracts. According to the stars, the earthly energy will make maintaining the status quo paramount this month. It will be best not to rush things; a steady and methodical approach will be necessary to break through any predicaments.

For the **Rat**, this month brings a significant improvement in fortune compared to last month, thanks to the help of benefactors. Although the influence of the clash with the *tai sui* remains, the help of benefactors may offer relief, potentially helping to resolve difficulties secretly. Their networks of contacts are poised to expand, and single Rats may meet someone suitable through introductions.

For the **Ox**, and other signs, the focus remains on navigating the clash between water and fire energies. The overall guidance for the month emphasizes caution in financial dealings and a patient approach to career and personal growth.

## Teacher's Tip
This article contains a lot of specific terminology related to astrology and feng shui. Note the use of "clash" (冲突) to describe the interaction between different elements (Water vs. Fire). In English, "clash" is often used for colors or personalities, but here it describes a metaphysical conflict. Also, notice the use of "prospects" to refer to future possibilities or chances of success.
