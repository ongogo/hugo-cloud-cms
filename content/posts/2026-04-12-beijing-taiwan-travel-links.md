## 🎯 核心词汇与表达 (Key Vocabulary & Expressions)

### 1. **Pledge** (v./n.)
- **Meaning:** to promise formally; a solemn promise.
- **中文:** 承诺；誓言
- **Collocations:** pledge to do sth / make a pledge / fulfill a pledge
- **Example:** **Beijing pledged to resume more cross-strait flights.**

### 2. **Resume** (v.)
- **Meaning:** to restart after a pause.
- **中文:** 恢复；重新开始
- **Memory tip:** “re-” = again; easy twin with “restart”.
- **Example:** **The airline will resume daily services next month.**

### 3. **Facilitate** (v.)
- **Meaning:** to make a process easier or smoother.
- **中文:** 促进；使便利
- **Synonyms:** ease, smooth, speed up
- **Example:** **The new app facilitates booking tickets.**

### 4. **Direct shipping route** (n. phrase)
- **Meaning:** sea lane allowing vessels to sail between two places without detour.
- **中文:** 直航航线
- **Usage:**多见于经贸、港口报道。

## 🔗 重点短语动词与连接词 (Phrasal Verbs & Connectors)

### 🟢 Phrasal Verbs
- **Ease up (on)** → 放宽（对...的限制）
  - *Usage:* "Officials will **ease up** entry procedures for Taiwan students."
- **Push forward (with)** → 推进（计划等）
  - *Usage:* "We must **push forward** cross-strait exchanges."

### 🔵 Connectors & High-scoring openers
- **"Following..."** → 继...之后（替换“after”更正式）
  - *Usage:* **Following the high-profile visit, Beijing announced...**
- **"In a move aimed at..."** → 旨在...的举措
  - *Usage:* **"In a move aimed at boosting people-to-people ties..."**
- **"Nevertheless / Nonetheless"** → 然而（比“but”高级）
  - *Usage:* **Political strains remain; nevertheless, practical links expand.**
