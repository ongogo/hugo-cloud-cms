## Article

An oil tanker sailing under the flag of Hong Kong has passed through the Strait of Hormuz and entered the Gulf of Oman, as a US blockade continues to target vessels entering or leaving Iranian ports, according to a maritime data firm.

The vessel, AVA 6, marks at least the second transit of a China-linked tanker through the waterway since the US military operation began. This movement is closely monitored by maritime data providers as it tests the parameters and enforcement of the ongoing US blockade targeting Iranian maritime trade.

## Phrases & Connectors 短语与连接词
1. **sailing under the flag of** - 挂...旗航行 - 用于描述船舶的登记国籍
 - *Context: \"An oil tanker sailing under the flag of Hong Kong has passed through the Strait of Hormuz...\"*
2. **passed through** - 通过/穿过 - 描述通过某个地理区域
 - *Context: \"...has passed through the Strait of Hormuz and entered the Gulf of Oman...\"*
3. **in test of** - 作为对...的测试 - 表示某行为旨在验证另一方的反应或效力
 - *Context: \"...passed through the Strait of Hormuz in test of US blockade\"*
4. **targeting vessels** - 针对船只 - 描述封锁或攻击的目标对象
 - *Context: \"...as a US blockade continues to target vessels entering or leaving Iranian ports...\"*
5. **marks at least the second transit** - 标志着至少第二次通过 - 用于统计次数并强调频率
 - *Context: \"AVA 6 passage marks at least the second transit of a China-linked tanker...\"*
6. **since the US military operation began** - 自美国军事行动开始以来 - 建立时间线参照点
 - *Context: \"...through the waterway since the US military operation began\"*
7. **according to a maritime data firm** - 根据一家海事数据公司的说法 - 引用信息来源
 - *Context: \"...entering or leaving Iranian ports, according to a maritime data firm.\"*
8. **entering or leaving** - 进入或离开 - 描述港口的出入状态
 - *Context: \"...targeting vessels entering or leaving Iranian ports...\"*

## Teacher's Tip
Focus on the phrase \"sailing under the flag of.\" In international maritime law, the \"flag state\" is the country where a ship is registered. This is crucial in geopolitical news because the flag determines which country's laws apply on the ship and how other nations treat it during blockades or conflicts.
